# Singapore Loan Rate Dashboard — Project Plan

## Objective

Build a banking analytics project that examines Singapore interest-rate trends, lending activity and the effect of different interest rates on loan affordability.

## Business Questions

- How have Singapore interest rates changed over time?
- Which periods experienced the largest increases or decreases?
- How have bank lending levels changed by industry?
- Which industries recorded the strongest lending growth?
- How do changes in interest rates affect monthly loan repayments?
- How much additional interest does a borrower pay when rates increase?

## Planned Dashboard Pages

### 1. Market Overview

- Latest available interest rate
- Historical interest-rate trend
- Year-on-year rate change
- Commercial lending totals
- Key market observations

### 2. Loan Affordability Calculator

- Loan amount input
- Interest-rate input
- Loan-tenure input
- Estimated monthly repayment
- Total repayment amount
- Total interest payable
- Comparison across multiple rate scenarios

### 3. Industry Lending Analysis

- Lending by industry
- Quarterly lending trends
- Industry share of total lending
- Fastest-growing borrowing sectors
